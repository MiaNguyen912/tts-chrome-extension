Privacy Policy – Speak Chrome Extension

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how the Speak Chrome extension handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension works entirely on your local device and does not connect to any external servers.

Permissions Used

  • contextMenus: Used to add a "Speak" option when you right-click selected text.
  • tts: Used to convert selected text into spoken audio using Chrome’s built-in text-to-speech engine.

No external or remote code is used. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.

Data Sharing

This extension does not share any data with third parties.

Security

Since no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data exposure through the extension.

Consent

By using the Speak extension, you agree to this privacy policy.
